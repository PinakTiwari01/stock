If you're working in Excel and need to remove empty cells, here’s a quick way to do it:
- Select the column where you want to delete empty cells.
- Press Ctrl + G to open the "Go To" dialog box.
- Click on Special, then select Blanks and hit OK—this highlights all empty cells.
- Right-click any of the selected cells and choose Delete.
- Select Shift cells up and click OK.
